Total Automation

Up & running in 5 Minutes.

No complex integration required. Voxali is designed to replace your front desk overnight.

1

Onboarding

You sign up for Voxali and input your salon's basic details: your address, operating hours, staff members, and the services you provide (along with prices and durations).

2

Your AI Number

The system instantly securely provisions a brand new, local phone number powered by Twilio. This number is directly wired into your new AI Receptionist, Aria.

3

Forwarding

You can either share this new AI number directly on your Instagram/Google Maps, OR simply set up unconditional Call Forwarding on your existing salon phone so every ring goes straight to Aria.

4

Live Execution

Aria operates implicitly. When a client calls, she accesses your Voxali calendar database in <0.5s via Edge Functions. She finds empty slots, negotiates the time securely, and writes the booking into your dashboard instantly.
