FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Voxali and how we bill.

How does the AI Receptionist work?

When a client calls your unique Voxali Twilio number, our AI answers in a natural voice. It can answer FAQs (like business hours, location, services) and even book appointments directly into your Voxali calendar. Your clients get 24/7 service without you lifting a finger.

What happens if I go over my monthly limits?

Don't worry, your AI receptionist will never stop answering calls! If you exceed your plan's included AI minutes, SMS, or emails, you will simply be charged a small overage fee at the end of the billing cycle (e.g., $0.25 per extra AI minute). Just like a standard cell phone data plan.

Do I need to buy a separate booking software?

No! Voxali is an all-in-one platform. Your subscription includes a complete Salon Booking Website, Staff Management, CRM, and Payment Processing. You can cancel your old, expensive booking software.

How do I get my salon's phone number?

Upon signing up, Voxali will automatically provision a new local phone number for your salon through Twilio. You can immediately start sharing this number with your clients, or forward your existing salon number to it.
